Welcome to On Track! Set the names and types of your goals below and get started tracking right away!

A "Binary" goal indicates a yes/no goal (e.g. did I go to the gym?).

A "Numeric" goal indicates a goal best measured on a numerical scale (e.g. how many hours of sleep did I get last night?)

Make sure your goals all have different names (this is case-insensitive, so "dishes" and "Dishes" will be counted as the same name).
